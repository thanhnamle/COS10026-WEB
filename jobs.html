Front-end Developer

Job reference number: F1200

Job Brief

You will focus on building and optimizing the user-facing parts of websites and applications, ensuring seamless user experiences through responsive and interactive designs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Use markup languages like HTML to create user-friendly web pages
  • Maintain and improve website
  • Design mobile-based features
  • Debug and troubleshoot UI/UX issues
  • Collaborate with back-end developers and web designers to improve usability

Skills and Qualifications

Essentials

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, UI/UX Design, or related field
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, or Vue.js)
  • Proven work experience as a Front-end developer
  • Strong understanding of responsive design principles
  • 2+ years of experience as a Front-End Developer

Preferable

  • Excellent analytical and multitasking skills
  • Experience with graphic design tools (e.g., Adobe XD, Figma)
  • Familiarity with version control tools like Git

Salary Range

  • $60,000 - $80,000 for entry-level positions
  • $70,000–$85,000 per year for mid-level developers with 3+ years of experience.

Benefits

General Benefits

  1. Health insurance, sick leave, and retirement plans
  2. Paid vacation and parental leave

Position-specific Benefits

  1. Company-sponsored attendance at design and development conferences (e.g., React Conf)
  2. Access to premium design tools and licenses (e.g., Figma Pro, Adobe Creative Cloud)
  3. Flexible work hours to accommodate creative workflows

Data Engineer

Job reference number: DE100

Job Brief

As a Data Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining scalable data pipelines and systems to ensure reliable data availability. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to transform raw data into valuable insights, enabling data-driven decisions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain data pipelines, ETL processes, and data integrations.
  • Collaborate with data scientists and analysts to optimize data models and workflows.
  • Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and security across all systems.
  • Monitor system performance and troubleshoot data-related issues.
  • Implement best practices for data engineering, including version control and CI/CD pipelines.

Skills and Qualifications

Essentials

  • Proficiency in SQL and database management systems (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL).
  • Experience with big data tools like Hadoop, Spark, or Kafka.
  • Strong programming skills in Python, Java, or Scala.
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  • Familiarity with data warehousing concepts and tools (e.g., Snowflake, Redshift).

Preferable

  • Experience with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Understanding of machine learning pipelines and MLOps.
  • Familiarity with streaming data technologies like Apache Flink or Storm.

Salary Range

  • $90,000 - $120,000 per year (entry-level to mid-level).
  • Up to $150,000+ for senior roles.

Benefits

General Benefits

  1. Health insurance, sick leave, and retirement plans
  2. Paid vacation and parental leave

Position-specific Benefits

  1. Access to cutting-edge tools and technology for data engineering.
  2. Opportunities for professional development and certifications.
